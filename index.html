Rock Scissor Paper

How to play: you (player) put one of the three option (rock, scissor or paper) into the text box. The bot will also give a choice. If your choice stronger than bot's choice, you score a win. Otherwise, you lose. If you have the same choice as the bot, you will tie the game. The score will be record, who ever score 3 first will win. And after 5 game, if your win games less than bot's win games, you lose.
